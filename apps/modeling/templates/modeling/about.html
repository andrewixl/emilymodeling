Who Am I?
My name is Emily Gray.

- I am a Body Positive Model who loves photography as well as modeling, looking for opprotunity and gaining experiance!
- I love taking both Portrait and Travel Shots whenever and wherever I can. I love working with a wide range of models to promote diversity and all body types!
- I currently live in the Greater Seattle Area and would be very excited to model or take photos of you!

What is Being Body Positive? It'll blow your mind.

I consider myself to be a body positive model. I define a body positive model to be one who is accepting of their body and chooses to embrace a way of living that gives you "permission" to love, care for, and take pleasure in your body throughout your lifespan. I strive to be body positive model because I believe it is important for everyone to be accepting of their body, and I hope to inspire some to do the same as me. I strongly promote self love and acceptance of everybody!
